Carlos C. Manuel

Privacy and InfoSec Compliance Partner

Carlos “Titus” Manuel is the resident Privacy and Infomation Security Compliance Partner of VTI. He is responsible in ensuring that the products and operations of VTI are conformant to Philippine Law. He currently serves as President of the Philippine Computer Society (PCS), leading initiatives that advance the country’s ICT sector. He is the Founding Chairman of the National Association of Data Protection Officers of the Philippines (NADPOP), advocating for data privacy and responsible digital transformation. Previously, he served as Chairman of the Advisory Council for the PNP Information Technology Management Service (ITMS), where he strengthened collaboration between the ICT community and law enforcement.
