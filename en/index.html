WHO WE ARE

VEDA Technologies, Inc. (VTI) is a software startup based in the Philippines that is changing the way we do computing. The future is the composition, distillation, and compounding of information. We are developing fault-tolerant high-performance database and mapping systems that work at any scale.

RESEARCH

The future of intelligence is neither brute-force statistics nor complex data warehousing. Energy-hungry implementations do not and can not scale. True AI requires consciousness.
