WHO WE ARE

VEDA Technologies, Inc. (VTI) is a premier technology solutions provider specializing in the development of secure, scalable, and innovative digital platforms. We cater to businesses, educational institutions, non-government organizations (NGOs), and public sector agencies seeking to optimize operations, enhance information security, and accelerate digital transformation. VTI integrates the principles of people, process, and protocols into every solution.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to deliver secure, stable, and scalable software systems that address real-world challenges while enforcing global standards of privacy and security; and to foster strategic partnerships that champion automation and digital empowerment across all sectors.

OUR VISION

To be Southeast Asia's leading developer of secure and intelligent software platforms that empower businesses, uplift communities, and drive digital transformation with integrity and innovation.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know about working with VTI. Clear answers, no fluff.

What pricing models do you offer?

▼

We offer flexible options: Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) with subscription-based cloud access, On-Premises Licensing for one-time purchases to run on your infrastructure, and Enterprise Consultancy for custom enhancements and adoption support.

How long does implementation typically take?

▼

Timelines vary by scope. We follow a phased approach: Alpha Testing with select partners, Beta Testing with pilot organizations, and Public Release for full commercial deployment. Core systems can be operational in 2-3 months, with enterprise solutions taking 4-6 months.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

▼

Absolutely. Whether it's legacy systems, third-party APIs, or existing databases, we ensure seamless integration. Our solutions are built to work harmoniously with your current infrastructure.

Do you provide training and ongoing support?

▼

Yes. We provide comprehensive training for your team, complete documentation, and structured support packages. Your success is our priority—we're with you long after deployment through quarterly updates and dedicated assistance.

Can we start with a pilot before full deployment?

▼

Definitely. We recommend phased rollouts and pilot programs. Start small, validate the impact, then scale confidently. This approach minimizes risk and proves ROI before major investment.

Where can we deploy your solutions?

▼

Your choice: Cloud-based for flexibility and scalability, or On-Premises for complete control and security. We support hybrid deployments too, giving you the best of both worlds.

How secure are your systems?

▼

Security is non-negotiable. We enforce global standards of privacy and security at every layer. Your sensitive data never touches third-party AI services—it stays airtight within your infrastructure.

What industries do you serve?

▼

We serve diverse sectors: Security Agencies, Utilities, Manufacturing, Healthcare, Government, NGOs, SMEs, Large Enterprises, Hotels, Restaurants, Power Distribution, and more. If you need secure, scalable systems, we've got you covered.

Who has worked on your technology?

▼

Our Chief Scientist brings 30 years of experience and has collaborated with scientists and engineers from MIT, Carnegie Mellon, University of Illinois CSL, and École Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications. World-class expertise, Filipino innovation.
