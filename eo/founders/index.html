Rommel Martínez

Chief Executive Officer | Chief Scientist

Co-founder of VTI, Rommel leads product vision and technical strategy with a focus on building secure, scalable, and innovative technology platforms.

With thirty years of combined professional experience in software engineering, systems administration, and programming language theory, he merges his technical skills with his acumen in leading a world-class software company.

His current research lies in understanding computationalism, the mind, knowledge, and reasoning. He has previously worked with scientists and engineers from the Coordinated Science Lab at the University of Illinois, École Nationale Supérieure des Télécommunications, MIT, and Carnegie Mellon University.

He is a polyglot who speaks and writes in five languages. In his spare time, he plays golf with his wife and son, and competes in sport shooting.

Dr. Jeanne Marie Ciño Martínez

Chief Operating Officer

Co-founder of VTI, Dr. Jem leads operations, partnerships, and strategic growth initiatives.

She commenced her leadership journey at a young age and assumed diverse and proficient management roles within her dental practice and several esteemed dental organizations.

Her keen interest in software development fostered a strong affinity for technology. Complementing her extensive experience in organizational leadership, marketing acumen, financial proficiency, and creative conceptualization, she positions herself as a valuable asset in devising optimal strategies for the company's growth.

In her leisure time, she engages in activities such as playing golf with her husband and son. Additionally, she pursues interests in writing, voice acting, and graphics design.
