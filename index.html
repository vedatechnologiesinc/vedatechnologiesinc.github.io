Our Products

Secure, Scalable, and Smart platforms built for modern organizations.

SAMSys Web Platform

Where security management meets smart automation. SAMsys is an integrated platform that simplifies guard deployment, timekeeping, payroll, invoicing, and compliance. By centralizing operations in one secure system, it enables agencies to work efficiently, maintain accuracy, and ensure seamless compliance.

PPDSI Web Platform

A modern, responsive web app enhances the Philippine Pediatric Dental Society Inc.’s online presence and outreach. Built for accessibility and ease of use, it features an intuitive interface with engaging visuals. The platform connects with dentists effectively while reinforcing the organization’s professional identity.

Chi-Phi Web Platform

A digital catalog connecting businesses with reliable heavy equipment solutions. Chi-Phi streamlines machinery sourcing, customer inquiries, and trade partnerships between China and the Philippines—helping industries find the right machines faster, build strong supplier relationships, and operate with confidence.
